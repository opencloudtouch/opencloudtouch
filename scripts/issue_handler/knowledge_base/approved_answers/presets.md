# Preset Management

OpenCloudTouch supports managing your Bose SoundTouch speaker presets (favorites).

## Viewing Presets

Navigate to your speaker's detail page to see all 6 presets. Each preset shows:
- Station name
- Source (TuneIn, local library, etc.)
- Artwork (if available)

## Setting Presets

You can set presets through the web interface:
1. Play the desired station or content on your speaker
2. Long-press the preset slot you want to assign
3. The current content will be saved to that preset

## Limitations

- Bose SoundTouch speakers support exactly **6 presets** per speaker
- Preset content depends on the source (TuneIn, Spotify, etc.)
- Some sources may not support preset saving

## Common Issues

- **Preset not playing**: The station may have been removed or the URL changed
- **Preset shows wrong name**: Try clearing and re-setting the preset
- **Can't save preset**: Make sure content is currently playing before saving
